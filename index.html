Shauna Tetterbor, LMFT

Hi, I’m Shauna. I specialize in helping individuals and couples manage anxiety and improve relationships using cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT). My approach is warm, compassionate, and focused on practical strategies that create lasting change.

With over a decade of experience, I work with clients to build resilience, improve communication, and navigate life’s challenges. I believe healing starts with understanding, and I’m here to provide the support and tools you need to feel empowered and confident in your journey.
